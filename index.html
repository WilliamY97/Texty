Introducing Texty

A new app that makes use of simple SMS messaging to return a variety of content everywhere you go - without the Wi-Fi.

Video Demo

Open Source

Texty is an open source project licensed under the BSD License. This means that you can use it from our repo.

Fast & Lightweight

Texty is fast and lightweight and will allow you to serve high traffic websites on modest hardware.

Wi-Fi Agnostic

Develop your application without having to worry about which RDBMS it is going to be deployed on.

About Texty

Created at Pennapps Winter 2016. It allows users to make SMS requests and return a variety of different values. Get all the information you want without the need for Wi-Fi.
